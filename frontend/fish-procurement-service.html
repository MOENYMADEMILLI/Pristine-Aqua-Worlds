Fish & Invertebrate Procurement Services

Ensure a healthy and harmonious aquarium with our expert guidance and safe introduction of new aquatic inhabitants.

Bringing New Life to Your Aqua World

Adding new fish or invertebrates to an established aquarium can be exciting, but it's also a critical step that requires careful consideration and precise execution. Without proper selection and acclimation, new additions can introduce diseases or stress your existing aquatic community.

Pristine Aqua Worlds takes the guesswork and risk out of expanding your underwater family. We leverage our expertise to help you choose compatible, healthy specimens and ensure their smooth transition into their new home.

Compatibility & Selection Advice

We'll help you select fish and invertebrates that are compatible with your current inhabitants, tank size, and water parameters, preventing aggression or overcrowding issues.

Reputable Sourcing

We'll guide you to or directly source healthy, quarantined specimens from trusted local fish stores or reputable online suppliers, minimizing the risk of disease.

Stress-Free Acclimation

Our experts will perform a careful, species-appropriate acclimation process to minimize stress and shock, greatly increasing the chances of successful integration.

Quarantine Tank Guidance

We can advise on or set up a temporary quarantine tank, a vital step to isolate new arrivals and prevent potential diseases from spreading to your main display.
