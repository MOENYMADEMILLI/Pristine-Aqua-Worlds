Vacation Care for Your Aquarium

Travel with complete peace of mind, knowing your fish and aquatic ecosystem are in expert, caring hands.

Worry-Free Travel for Aquarium Owners

Planning a vacation or a business trip? The last thing you want to worry about is the health and well-being of your beloved fish and the condition of your aquarium. Relying on friends or automated feeders can sometimes fall short, leading to overfeeding, underfeeding, or undetected issues.

Pristine Aqua Worlds offers professional **Vacation Care services** designed to keep your "aqua world" thriving while you're away. We provide diligent feeding, water checks, and overall monitoring, ensuring you return to a healthy and beautiful tank.

Customized Feeding Schedules

We'll feed your fish according to their specific dietary needs and schedule, preventing over or underfeeding issues common during travel.

Daily/Scheduled Health Checks

During each visit, we'll visually inspect all fish for signs of disease or distress, and assess general water clarity and equipment function.

Water Top-Offs & Light Adjustments

We'll replenish evaporated water as needed and ensure your lighting schedule remains consistent for plants and corals.

Proactive Problem Detection

Our experienced eye can spot potential issues early, allowing for timely intervention if a problem arises, preventing minor issues from becoming major ones.
