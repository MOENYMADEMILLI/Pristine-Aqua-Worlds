Optimal Balance: The Bi-Weekly Advantage

Our **Bi-Weekly Care Plan** is a popular choice for many aquarium owners, offering an ideal balance between consistent professional maintenance and cost-effectiveness. With visits every two weeks, we ensure your tank remains healthy, clear, and vibrant without the constant commitment from you.

This plan is well-suited for most established **freshwater aquariums** and many **saltwater setups** that don't require the daily intricacies of a full reef system. We proactively address common issues, perform essential maintenance, and monitor your aquatic environment to keep it flourishing.

Regular Water Changes

Consistent 30-40% water changes and precise water conditioning to remove pollutants and replenish essential minerals.

Thorough Cleaning & Substrate Care

Comprehensive algae removal from glass, decor, and equipment surfaces, along with effective gravel/substrate vacuuming.

Comprehensive Water Parameter Testing

Detailed testing of key parameters (e.g., pH, Ammonia, Nitrite, Nitrate, Salinity for saltwater) to ensure optimal and stable conditions.

Fish & Plant Health Assessment

Careful observation of all fish, invertebrates, and live plants for signs of stress, disease, or deficiencies, with advice on solutions.

Filter & Equipment Maintenance

Thorough rinsing or replacement of filter media as needed, and full inspection of all equipment (heaters, pumps, lights) for peak performance.

Proactive Advice & Minor Adjustments

We provide ongoing advice on tank husbandry and make minor adjustments to maintain a balanced ecosystem, catching issues early.
