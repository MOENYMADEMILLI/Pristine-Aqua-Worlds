Our Comprehensive Aquarium Services

From initial design and setup to ongoing meticulous maintenance, we provide everything you need for a thriving aquatic environment.

Professional Tank Setup & Installation

Starting a new aquarium, or upgrading an existing one, can be a complex process. Let Pristine Aqua Worlds handle the intricacies, ensuring a beautiful and healthy foundation for your aquatic life.

Consultation & Design

We work with you to understand your vision, space, and desired aquatic inhabitants. We'll advise on tank size, filtration, lighting, heating, and suitable decor.

Equipment Procurement & Assembly

Don't get overwhelmed by choices. We can help you source the best quality equipment, then deliver and expertly assemble all components right in your home.

Aquascaping & Filling

We'll create a stunning underwater landscape with substrate, rocks, driftwood, and live plants (if desired), followed by careful filling and initial water conditioning.

Initial Cycling & Acclimation Guidance

We'll initiate the crucial nitrogen cycle and provide clear instructions for proper cycling. We can also assist with the safe acclimation of your new fish.

Starting Setup Fees:
  • **Basic Freshwater Tanks (20-50 Gallons):** $150 - $300
  • **Mid-size Freshwater Tanks (50-100 Gallons):** $250 - $500
  • **Large/Complex Freshwater Tanks (100+ Gallons, planted/custom):** $400 - $800+
  • **Basic Saltwater Tanks (20-50 Gallons):** $300 - $600 (Higher due to specialized equipment & water)
  • **Mid-size Saltwater Tanks (50-100 Gallons):** $500 - $1000+
  • **Large/Reef Saltwater Tanks (100+ Gallons):** Custom Quote

Fees are for labor and expertise; equipment, livestock, and consumables are additional and typically purchased by the client.

Reliable Maintenance Plans

Keep your aquarium sparkling clean and your aquatic inhabitants healthy with our professional, recurring maintenance services. Choose the plan that best fits your needs and schedule.

Weekly Care Plan

**Ideal for:** High-demand setups (e.g., heavily stocked, reef tanks), or for those who want ultimate peace of mind.

  • 25-30% Water Change & Water Conditioning
  • Algae Removal (Glass, Decor)
  • Substrate Vacuuming
  • Filter Media Rinse/Cleaning
  • Water Parameter Testing (pH, Ammonia, Nitrite, Nitrate)
  • Fish Health & Behavior Observation
  • Equipment Check (Heater, Filter, Lights)

Starting from **$50 per visit**

Bi-Weekly Care Plan

**Ideal for:** Most established freshwater tanks and some less demanding saltwater setups.

  • 30-40% Water Change & Water Conditioning
  • Algae Removal & Substrate Vacuuming
  • Filter Media Cleaning/Replacement (as needed)
  • Comprehensive Water Parameter Testing
  • Fish & Plant Health Assessment
  • Equipment Inspection

Starting from **$70 per visit**

Monthly Care Plan

**Ideal for:** Heavily planted tanks, lightly stocked freshwater tanks, or low-maintenance setups.

  • 40-50% Water Change & Water Conditioning
  • Thorough Algae Removal & Substrate Cleaning
  • Deep Filter Cleaning/Media Exchange
  • Extensive Water Parameter Testing & Adjustment
  • Detailed Fish & Plant Health Check
  • Full Equipment Functionality Check

Starting from **$85 per visit**

Pricing varies based on tank size (gallons), freshwater vs. saltwater, complexity, and specific needs. Contact us for a precise quote. Minimum charge applies for smaller tanks.
