A convenient and effective solution for established, lower-maintenance aquariums to maintain health and clarity.

Flexible & Efficient: The Monthly Advantage

Our **Monthly Care Plan** is an ideal option for aquarium owners with well-established freshwater tanks, lightly stocked setups, or those that naturally require less frequent intervention. With a professional visit once every four weeks, this plan provides comprehensive cleaning and vital health checks, ensuring your "aqua world" remains vibrant and healthy without over-servicing.

This plan is perfect for hobbyists who enjoy some hands-on interaction with their tank but want to offload the major, time-consuming maintenance tasks to experts. We focus on in-depth cleaning and thorough inspections to keep your aquarium balanced and beautiful.

Substantial Water Changes

A thorough 40-50% water change with precise water conditioning to significantly reduce accumulated nitrates and refresh the aquatic environment.

Deep Cleaning & Detritus Removal

Extensive algae removal from all surfaces, detailed gravel/substrate vacuuming, and cleaning of decor to maintain pristine clarity.

Comprehensive Water Testing & Adjustment

In-depth testing of all crucial water parameters (e.g., pH, Ammonia, Nitrite, Nitrate, GH, KH, Salinity for saltwater) with expert recommendations for adjustment.

Detailed Health & Ecosystem Check

A thorough assessment of all fish, invertebrates, and plants for any signs of disease, stress, or nutrient deficiencies, and advice on preventative measures.

Full Filter & Equipment Overhaul

Detailed cleaning and, if necessary, replacement of filter media. Comprehensive inspection of all pumps, heaters, lighting, and other equipment to ensure optimal functionality.

Expert Guidance & Recommendations

We'll provide a full report on your aquarium's health and offer personalized advice on feeding, stocking, and any improvements for long-term vitality.
